Three kinds of flower tea gift set
Put a flower path in a drop of flower tea. Flood takes a long time to make the best handmade flower tea. Because it keeps the beauty of the flower as it is, the color and fragrance of the flower tea are abundant, making it feel like a flower path. It is a package design that expresses the characteristics of the flowered flower car using beautiful landscape graphics and geostructure.

Walk the flower road. In order to feel like entering the landscape of the flower path as soon as the package is opened, we maximized the identity of the float using sleeve and pitch technique. It presents the experience of making the eyes happy with the package as well as the taste and aroma of the flowered flower tea.

Bees and honey and a beehive cake gift set
It is natural and eco-friendly and designed with graphics of tree texture to appeal organic products, showing the feeling of wood. It is a package to express the object of the beehive, and to give consumers the feeling that they have received the beehive as it is.

To further highlight the tree texture of the package, the kraft paper was used to highlight the roughness of the tree, not only the visual part but also the touch. [It gives a luxurious feeling by using Black Color for the natural feeling of *kraft paper.]

At the corner of the package, hinge graphics were placed on the wooden joint and the foldable portion of the top, the text gave a tanning feeling around the text to give the impression that the flame was printed on the wooden board, and black foil was added to the detail.

The paper structure is easy to load the handle, easy to use for gifts and mobility, and to prevent the product from being folded. Overall, the color of the package is a package box design that emphasizes luxury by catching it with a heavy dark tone.

SSG E-Mart Shoppie
Shinsegae E-Mart has entered the overseas online market and has become the best mobile shopping platform in Southeast Asia with the attention of the Taiwanese market due to the demand of the market where the snack culture has developed. Accomets studied the design of the carton box that will be remembered by overseas consumers who will be exposed to Korean brands and products through the show. The most important factor was to recognize the brand and focus on the design with the idea.

While studying various concepts and ideas, we focused on user experience-oriented perspectives that use boxes in everyday life and then fold them, store them, or discard them. When folded, we completed a package design that featured a mini container box with the E-Mart logo on it and a Shappy truck with containers when folded. This package, which contains the brand identity of Chopi and E-Mart, is currently meeting many consumers overseas.
